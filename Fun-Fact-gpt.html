Did You Know?

  • The Eiffel Tower can grow more than 6 inches in summer due to metal expansion.
  • Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found 3000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs, still edible!
  • Sharks existed before trees. Sharks have been around for over 400 million years.
  • The heart of a blue whale is so large that a human could swim through its arteries!
  • An octopus has three hearts. Two pump blood to the gills, and one pumps it to the rest of the body.
